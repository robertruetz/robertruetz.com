My name is Robert, and I have a problem: too many interests, not enough time to properly pursue them all. Professionally, I'm an audio editor, recording and editing audio for Walsh Media, Inc. I've also dabbled in video editing, although I don't claim to be an expert. However, in my spare time (and even on the clock, shhhh), I love to code. My first experiences with coding on a practical level came when I was faced with repetitive audio-related tasks that could only logically be solved with a C# script in Sound Forge (audio editing software). From there I started playing around with Java, Ruby and Python, but never managed to attain a high level of proficiency. I've now shelved all of that for a bit while I wrap my noodle around this whole web development thing. So, herein you will find my adventures with web dev. I like to learn. What could possibly go wrong? Well, you'll get to find out if you follow the evolution of this website.
Cheers,
Rob.
